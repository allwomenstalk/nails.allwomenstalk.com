# Fab Inspos for Girls Who Want to Get on Bubble Manicure Bandwagon! ...

A bubble manicure isn't a nail art design featuring bubbles. It's the latest craze in the nail world and involves dropping many puddles of polish on the nail, forming a "bubble" or hump on your nail. Intrigued? It's definitely a commitment and may not make everyday tasks all that convenient. But hey, fashion over comfort, right? Here are some great examples of a bubble manicure.
