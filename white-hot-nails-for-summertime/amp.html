Frequently Asked Questions

Why are white nails perfect for the summer?
White nails are like the little black dress of nail art—they go with everything! They look super chic against tanned skin and give off those beachy, carefree vibes we all love in the summer. Plus, they're a great base if you want to add some cute designs or sparkles!
What are some trendy white nail designs for summer?
Oh, so many! You could go for classic all-white nails for a clean, minimalist look. Or you could jazz it up with white with gold accents, subtle geometric lines, or even some cute summer-themed nail stickers. French tips with a twist, like glitter or neon colors, are also super popular.
Do white nails look good on all skin tones?
Absolutely! White nails are universally flattering. They pop against darker skin tones and give a polished look to lighter skin tones. It's really all about finding the right shade of white that you love—whether it's a bright, stark white or a softer, more muted off-white.
How do I keep my white nails looking fresh all summer?
A good quality top coat is your best friend! It'll keep your white nails looking shiny and prevent chipping. Also, make sure to reapply it every few days. And, of course, try not to use your nails as tools—no picking, prying, or scraping to keep them pristine!
Can I do white nails at home, or should I go to a salon?
You can totally do them at home! All you need is a good white nail polish, a steady hand, and some patience. If you're into nail art, you might want to get some small brushes or nail art tools. But if you want something more intricate or just want to treat yourself, definitely hit up a salon!
