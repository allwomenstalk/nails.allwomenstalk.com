# 7 Best Nail Art Kits to Try This Summer ...

Since nail design is a hot trend right now, many companies are creating nail art kits that feature an array of design elements. Ciate is well known for their caviar manicure kits, but they've also developed a few different ones over the years, along with Sally Hansen and other brands. Additionally, many kits are simple to use and just as fun as painting your nails with colored nail polish (but a bit messier!). That being said, do yourself a favor and pick out one of the nail art kits listed below and make sure to try it out this summer!

## 1. Ciate Sequined Manicure Kit

![Ciate Sequined Manicure Kit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nails/2013/07/1_ciate-sequined-manicure-kit_450x450.jpg)

Even though Ciate is known for their caviar manicure kits and rightfully so, a few of their other nail art kits are becoming increasingly popular. Make your nails sparkle like disco balls with this sequined manicure! You get to choose from two color options: pink or multi-color. If you're looking for a strikingly glam look, you just have to pick this kit up! Besides the sequins, this kit comes with a bottle of Sequin Glitter Grip and a little black brush. You can snatch it up at Sephora.

## 2. Sally Hansen Salon Insta-Gel Strips Kit

![Sally Hansen Salon Insta-Gel Strips Kit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nails/2013/07/2_sally-hasen-salon-insta-gel-strips-kit_600x499.jpg)

If you're a fan of at-home gel manicure products, you can't pass this product up! Not only is this starter kit super easy to use (it only requires 2 steps!), but it also includes a LED light, which is a total bonus considering you don't have to buy one separately! Just make sure to follow the directions exactly (maybe even giving your gel manicure a little more time to cure than the kit suggests) and apply the gel polish generously, especially over the tip of the nail, so the manicure lasts longer and doesn't chip! You can pick this kit up at Sally Hansen.

## 3. Ciate Sugar Caviar Manicure Kit

![Ciate Sugar Caviar Manicure Kit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nails/2013/07/3_ciate-sugar-caviar-manicure-kit_450x450.jpg)

This manicure kit is very similar to Ciate's traditional caviar manicure except that the color scheme reminds me of sprinkles! You get to choose from three different pastel color combinations. Each set comes with a nail polish, the caviar peals and a funnel. You can get your hands on this kit at Sephora.

## 4. Elegant Touch Nail Art Glitter & Stud Kit

![Elegant Touch Nail Art Glitter & Stud Kit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nails/2013/07/4_elegant-touch-nail-art-glitter-stud-kit_600x423.jpg)

This kit is perfect for the ladies who love bling! It comes with fifty studs, 2 glitter-filled puffers, a nail art applicator and a protective sealer. Bedazzle your fingernails to your heart's content! You can purchase this kit at Ulta.

## 5. Ciate Chalkboard Manicure Kit

![Ciate Chalkboard Manicure Kit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nails/2013/07/5_ciate-chalkboard-manicure-kit_450x450.jpg)

I found this manicure kit so darn cool that I just had to share! This kit turns your nails into mini chalkboards! It comes with four colored chalkboard pens, a top coat and chalkboard paint. I can't wait to try this one out! How about you? You can pick one up at Sephora.

## 6. Essence Nail Art Decoration Kit

![Essence Nail Art Decoration Kit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nails/2013/07/6_essence-nail-art-decoration-kit_400x400.jpg)

Calling all glitter fans! This kit features various types of glitter, ranging from rhinestones to tiny glitter fragments and flakes. Get ready to sparkle, ladies! You can get your hands on this glitzy kit at Ulta.

## 7. Ciate Velvet Manicure Kit

![Ciate Velvet Manicure Kit](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nails/2013/07/7_ciate-velvet-manicure-kit_450x411.jpg)

You can purchase the kit in two colors: purple or gray. Once applied, you get a velvety texture similar to suede! The kit comes with a nail polish, a little black brush, crushed velvet powder and a little tray. Get ready for some classy looking nails, ladies! Check it out at Sephora.

So, ladies, which one intrigues you the most? Are you planning on trying any of them out? If so, take a moment and let me know!
