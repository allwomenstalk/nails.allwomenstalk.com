# This is Why You Should Try Dip Dying Your Fingernails ...

You're probably pretty familiar with dip dyed hair, right? You can easily copy that look on your fingernails for a look that's colorful and unique. Obviously, you aren't going to dip your fingers into a bottle of nail polish, but you can use the brush to make your nails look that way without the mess and waste. Need some inspiration? Have a look at how these fabulous ladies have done it. I think you'll agree that it's pretty easy once you give it a try. Have fun!
