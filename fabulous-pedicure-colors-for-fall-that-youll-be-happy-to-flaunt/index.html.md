# 7 Fabulous Pedicure Colors for Fall That Youll Be Happy to Flaunt ...

Does the coming of fall really mean we need to put our pretty feet away or can it be that there are [pedicure colors](https://nails.allwomenstalk.com/nail-polish-colors-men-love-on-women/) for fall that will still have us showing off our feet in peep toe pumps? I’ve currently seen boots for fall and even those have peep toes so I do believe we ladies should keep up the polishing. You don’t need to do the whole pedicure and massaging of your feet but maybe just the surface like your nails. If you don’t want to hide your toes yet, here are 7 fabulous pedicure colors for fall that are so in!

## 1. Navy Blue

![Navy Blue](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/01040009_8314_374x483.jpg)

I know it’s still summer but I am currently rocking the navy blue of the pedicure colors for fall. At Anna Sui’s fall 2012 fashion show, the chosen nail polish was an dark royal blue. The color is not quite as edgy as a black, but blue looks especially sophisticated on square-meets-oval nail shapes. The color will have you looking sophisticated and stylish.

## 2. Deep Plum

![Deep Plum](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/01040012_4774_600x600.jpg)

Whether you go for a candy-color version like the one at Ruffian or a deep plum, try a purple nail on for size. It’s an elegant update for fall. It's an unexpected, darker shade that's more exciting than ordinary black nails, but not quite as blatantly girly as a bright pink. It’s the color for the ladies who are crazy about the neon but know that neon doesn’t exactly work for fall. This color will look incredible with a military green outfit, I can see it now!

## 3. Blood Red

![Blood Red](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/01040018_1724_359x434.jpg)

Is it any wonder that fall’s hottest runway color is also making its way to your nails? As seen at Emporio Armani, the dark shade perfectly complements the season’s leather accessories. Dramatic and chic, this fall’s merlot-inspired polish trend is one that’s sure to stick around for seasons to come. It perfectly complements almost all of fall’s neutral color palettes and gives your nails just the right dash of color. Check out Butter London’s La Moss Nail Lacquer-an essential at Rachel Roy and Dannijo during Fashion Week FW13, you can’t go wrong with this polish. It's a bit more mysterious than your typical red, and the darker shade couples perfectly with cozy fall flannels!

## 4. Classic Nude

![Classic Nude](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/01040010_1458_599x367.jpg)

While I’ll always love the crazy nail art trends, occasionally a palette cleanser is in order. Enter classic nudes like those spotted at Carolina Herrera. Finish with a clear topcoat for extra shine.

## 5. Burnt Orange

![Burnt Orange](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/01040009_8254_258x441.jpg)

Christian Cota was onto something when he sent a burnt orange nail look down the runway for autumn. Orange nail polish doesn’t have to be reserved for Halloween. Le Metier de Beaute takes this camp fire color and makes it one of the trendiest colors for the fall. Slightly darker than candy-corn orange, Le Metier’s Clockwork Orange is two parts playful and delicious. Why not match your nails to the fall foliage.

## 6. Gray

![Gray](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/01040009_1298_600x600.jpg)

Consider concrete gray, like the one Elie Saab used, your new favorite neutral. Although it more eye-catching than nude or white, it won’t interfere with your everyday style.

## 7. Teal

![Teal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/09/01040010_6448_350x350.jpg)

Teal is simply one of those colors that works year-round. It packs a punch when worn with khaki and browns like Tracy Reese and also has a magical ability to spark daydreams about those beachside days of summer, even in the dead of winter.

These pedicure colors for fall have me excited to do my nails every day! I am so obsessed with the colors because they are so chic yet appropriate for the changing of the leaves. What do you think of these pedicure colors for fall? Are they too dark for you or just the right amount of fabulous?
