Frequently Asked Questions

What are Shellac nails?
Shellac nails are a gel-polish hybrid that offers the shine of gel and the ease of polish, lasting longer than regular nail polish.
How long do Shellac nails typically last?
They usually last about 2 to 3 weeks without chipping when applied and cared for properly.
Is Shellac nail application damaging to my natural nails?
When applied and removed correctly, Shellac is gentle and doesn't damage natural nails.
Can I apply Shellac nails at home?
Yes, with the right kit and proper curing under a UV or LED lamp, you can apply Shellac nails at home.
How do I remove Shellac nails safely?
Soak a cotton ball in acetone, place it on the nail, wrap it in foil, and wait about 10-15 minutes before gently scraping off.
Are Shellac nails suitable for nail art designs?
Absolutely, Shellac is great for various nail art styles due to its smooth and durable finish.
Can I change colors frequently with Shellac nails?
While you can change colors, it's best to wait until the Shellac is removed to avoid nail damage.
Do Shellac nails dry instantly?
Yes, Shellac nails dry instantly under UV or LED light, eliminating smudging concerns.
Are Shellac nails waterproof?
Shellac nails are water-resistant but not completely waterproof, so avoid prolonged submersion.
Can Shellac nails cause allergic reactions?
Allergic reactions are rare but possible; it's important to test for sensitivity before a full application.
