Frequently Asked Questions

What are the latest salon nail trends?
Current trends include minimalist designs, pastel colors, and glossy finishes for a chic look.
How often should I get my nails done in a salon?
Generally, every 2-3 weeks is ideal to maintain healthy and polished nails.
What nail shape is most popular right now?
Almond and coffin shapes are very popular for a stylish, feminine appearance.
Are gel nails safe for my natural nails?
When applied and removed properly, gel nails are generally safe and can protect your nails.
How can I maintain my salon nails at home?
Use cuticle oil daily, avoid harsh chemicals, and wear gloves during cleaning to keep nails healthy.
What should I do if I have weak or brittle nails?
Ask your nail technician for strengthening treatments and choose nail polishes with nourishing ingredients.
Can I get nail art on both acrylic and natural nails?
Yes, nail art can be applied on acrylic, gel, or natural nails to suit your preference.
Is it okay to change nail colors frequently?
Yes, just make sure to give your nails a break occasionally to avoid damage.
How do I choose the right nail salon for me?
Look for clean facilities, experienced technicians, and positive reviews to ensure a great experience.
What should I avoid after getting a manicure?
Avoid using nails to open things and prolonged exposure to water to maintain polish and nail health.
