# 7 Turquoise Nail Polishes to Shake up Your Look ...

Turquoise nail polishes are all over the place these days, which is great news if you love this wonderful shade as much as I do. Turquoise is a hot color and adding it to your fingernails is an easy way to give it a try. There’s nothing more fabulous than turquoise toenails sticking out of your sandals too. So, go ahead and give these great turquoise nail polishes a try and you’ll see exactly what I mean. Once you try turquoise, you’ll never go back!

## 1. Turquoise and Caicos from Essie

![Turquoise and Caicos from Essie](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uo/fj/zuofo3zf_130x276.jpg)

This isn’t your standard shade of turquoise, but you won’t want to pass it up. It’s a color that will bring to mind the ocean and you’ll love the way it looks with a bright white sundress or even your favorite pair of jeans. This will quickly become your favorite of the many turquoise nail polishes that grace store shelves.

## 2. Turquoise from Pop

![Turquoise from Pop](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/t9/od/o3101bda_300x256.jpg)

If turquoise is your favorite color, this bottle is the one for you. It’s the classic turquoise color you always think of and it gives all your favorite outfits a great pop of color anytime you need it. Wear this shade alone or mix and match it with other colors to create nail art that will be your best masterpiece.

## 3. Poole Nail Lacquer from Butter London

![Poole Nail Lacquer from Butter London](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jc/qj/slsbgdpi_480x360.jpg)

This wonderful robin’s egg shade of turquoise is a bit more subtle than your average turquoise nail polish, but that doesn’t mean it won’t hold a spot in your top shades. I love this color with virtually any other color, which means painting your nails at the beginning of the week and matching it to your clothes for days.

## 4. Constant Caribbean by Cover Girl

![Constant Caribbean by Cover Girl](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/z3/ho/g6nwiqku_300x334.jpg)

This color is perfect for reliving your favorite beach vacation. The name alone makes it sound so tropical and warm, but the way it looks on your nails will leave you smiling all day long. Of course, it’s also turquoise so you can indulge your love of this color where you can see it anytime you need a good dose of bright color in your day.

## 5. Now You Sea Me from Loreal

![Now You Sea Me from Loreal](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ri/ly/b7ji9awc_300x298.jpg)

Did you see what they did there with the name of the polish? Yep, this nail color is reminiscent of the ocean and you’re going to love it. It’s a great mixture of blue and turquoise, which creates a shade you can’t find just anywhere. This is the perfect choice if you wear jeans all the time.

## 6. Hurray Huarache by Venique

![Hurray Huarache by Venique](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b5/g8/nar0xguf_500x498.jpg)

I can’t explain the name of this nail polish, but I can totally get behind the color. Not only do you get a great turquoise color with this bottle, but it also has a hint of sparkle to glam up your manicure. After all, who doesn’t love some glitter? This is a great polish for a night on the town, but it isn’t so dramatic that you can’t get away with it during the day too.

## 7. Can’t Find My Czechbook by OPI

![Can’t Find My Czechbook by OPI](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/jl/nu/wt41oqqm_106x250.jpg)

Again, the name is strange, but the color more than makes up for it. OPI is touted as being some of the best nail polish around. I can’t say one way or the other, but I love it. This color is a fun turquoise blend that you won’t be able to get enough of. Use it for any occasion and everyone will compliment your great style.

Do you wear turquoise nail polish? Do these great shades inspire you? I love to shake things up with turquoise nails now and then and I think you will too.
