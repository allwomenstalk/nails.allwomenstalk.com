Frequently Asked Questions

What are some trendy nail designs for prom?
Oh, there are so many trendy options! From sparkly bling nails to classy champagne hues, and even nails that match your dress color—like green, navy, or light blue. It's all about what makes you feel fabulous!
Do you have nail ideas for a black prom dress?
Totally! A black dress really lets you play around with your nail design. You can go with anything from elegant silver or sleek navy blue nails, to even a bold champagne color. And don’t forget adding some bling for that wow factor!
Any suggestions for nails to go with a navy blue dress?
Absolutely! You can match your nails to your navy blue dress for a chic look, or you can opt for something like silver or even a light blue to complement the dress. A bit of bling can also elevate the look, making it perfect for prom night.
Can I try light blue nails for prom?
Why not?! Light blue nails are super cute and trendy for prom. They can be subtly glamorous or completely eye-catching, depending on how you style them. Great choice!
How can I make my prom nails really stand out?
Bling it up! Add some sparkly gems or go for a metallic finish like silver or champagne. Don't be afraid to experiment with bold colors that contrast or match your dress. And remember, the more fabulous you feel, the more you'll shine!
