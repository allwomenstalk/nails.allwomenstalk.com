Frequently Asked Questions

What are squared short nails?
Squared short nails are nail shapes with straight edges and a flat, squared-off tip, perfect for a neat and modern look.
Are squared short nails easy to maintain?
Yes, their short length and simple shape make them low-maintenance and less prone to breakage.
What nail polish colors suit squared short nails?
Bold and bright colors look great, as well as neutral tones like nude and soft pinks.
Can I do nail art on squared short nails?
Absolutely! Their flat tips provide a great canvas for various nail art designs.
Are squared short nails good for everyday wear?
Yes, they're practical and stylish, ideal for both professional and casual settings.
How do I shape my nails into squared short nails?
File the sides straight and flatten the tip to create the signature squared shape, keeping nails short.
Do squared short nails suit all hand shapes?
They complement most hand shapes and can help fingers appear longer and more elegant.
Can I grow out my nails from squared short to longer length?
Yes, squared short nails are a great starting point to grow your nails out while keeping a clean look.
What are the benefits of short nails vs. long nails?
Short nails are less likely to break and are more hygienic, while still offering style versatility.
How often should I trim my squared short nails?
Trim or file them every 1-2 weeks to maintain their shape and neat appearance.
