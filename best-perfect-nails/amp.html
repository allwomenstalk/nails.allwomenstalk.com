Frequently Asked Questions

How can I achieve perfect nails at home?
Start with clean nails, use a base coat, apply two thin layers of polish, and finish with a top coat for durability.
What are some tips to prevent nail breakage?
Keep nails moisturized, avoid harsh chemicals, and don't use them as tools to reduce breakage.
How often should I trim or file my nails?
Trim or file your nails every 1-2 weeks to maintain shape and prevent splits.
Are nail strengthening treatments effective?
Yes, nail strengtheners can help reinforce weak nails when used regularly.
What's the best way to remove gel polish safely?
Soak cotton in acetone, apply it on nails, wrap with foil, and gently scrape off after 10-15 minutes.
How do I maintain healthy cuticles?
Moisturize daily and avoid cutting cuticles to prevent infections and promote nail growth.
Can diet affect nail health?
Absolutely! Eating protein-rich foods and vitamins like biotin supports stronger, healthier nails.
Should I avoid nail polish sometimes?
Giving your nails a break allows them to breathe and prevents discoloration and brittleness.
What nail shapes are trendy and easy to maintain?
Oval and square shapes are popular and practical for everyday wear.
How can I fix a broken nail quickly?
Use a tea bag or silk wrap with clear nail glue as a quick repair before trimming or reshaping.
