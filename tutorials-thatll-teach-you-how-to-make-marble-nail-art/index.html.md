# 7 Tutorials Thatll Teach You How to Make Marble Nail Art ...

You don't want to paint your nails a plain color if you're going to a big event. There's nothing exciting about that. You need to think outside of the box. If you're interested in trying something new and have a decent chunk of time on your hands, here are a few different tutorials that'll teach you how to create marble nail art that everyone is going to love:

## 1. Black and White Marble Nails

You should start by painting your nails white. Then you should prep your nails by placing some tape around each of them, so that you don't get your fingers covered with polish. When you're finished with the process, you can pull that tape right off, which makes for easy cleanup. It's better than scrubbing your hands to get the color off.

## 2. Red and Blue Marble Nails

Creating water marble nail art can take a long time, so you don't have to do the pattern on every single one of your nails. You can try painting the majority of your nails a solid color and then do the marble nail art on one finger per hand. That way, you won't waste too much time, but you'll still look fabulous and receive plenty of compliments.

## 3. Pink Marble Nails

Even though Valentine's Day is over, you can still create this gorgeous nail art. It'll make you look fun and flirty. This video even gives you little tips, like to use filtered water instead of tap water if you're having trouble creating your designs. Of course, you should also remember to clean up your excess polish whenever you finish one nail and want to move on to the next.

## 4. Yellow, Blue, and Pink Marble Nails

Make sure you use nail polish that is new, so that it hasn't thickened. That's how you'll get the best results possible. You should also make sure that the water you're using is room temperature. Otherwise, it's not going to work.

## 5. Bumblebee Marble Nails

If you want to create bumblebee inspired marble nail art, then you need a lot of yellow nail polish and only a little bit of black. All you have to do is drop that polish into a cup of water. The woman in the video suggests using 8 drops of yellow, 2 drops of black, and 2 more drops of yellow. Once you do that, start creating stripes! The key is to overlap your strokes.

## 6. Neon Flowers Marble Nails

This tutorial proves that you can use as many colors as possible, and your nails will still look amazing! So if you can't settle on two or three shades, you can use all of the nail polish that you own. The more colors, the merrier!

## 7. Purple Swirl Marble Nails

Even though this video isn't in English, you should be able to understand everything that's going on. You're basically following the same process that you've learned about in the previous videos. The only difference is that you're using different colors and using a dotting tool to draw a spiral in the water.

Even though marble nail art can take a little while to create, it'll end up looking amazing once you're finished. Which of these color combos do you think is the cutest?
