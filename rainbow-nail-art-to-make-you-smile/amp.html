17 Neon Water Marble Nails

***

Neon water marble nails are a unique and eye-catching way to show off your style. This type of nail art is created by combining different colors of nail polish to create a marbled effect. The result is an abstract, water-like pattern that is sure to turn heads.

To create this look, start by painting your nails with a white base coat. This will help the colors of the marbling to stand out. Next, fill a cup with warm water and add drops of different colored polishes. Swirl the colors together with a toothpick or a pin to create the marbling effect. Then, dip your nails into the water and use a paper towel to blot off any excess polish.

Once your nails have dried, apply a top coat to seal in the design and make it last longer. For an extra pop of color, try adding a few rhinestones or glitter pieces to the design.
