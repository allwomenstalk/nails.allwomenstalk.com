Frequently Asked Questions

What are some unique French tip designs?
Oh, there are so many! You can try adding glitter to the tips, using bright colors instead of the classic white, or even drawing small patterns on the accent nail. Seriously, the possibilities are endless!
Can I add an accent nail to my French manicure?
Absolutely, you can! Accent nails are a great way to add a little extra oomph to your French manicure. Whether it’s a different color, a sparkly glitter, or a cute design, it’s totally up to you.
How do I make my French manicure last longer?
To keep those fabulous tips looking fresh, make sure you use a high-quality base coat and top coat. Also, try to avoid using your nails as tools and keep them away from harsh chemicals. Trust me, a little care goes a long way!
Are French tips only for long nails?
Not at all! You can rock French tips on both short and long nails. In fact, short nails with a clean French tip can look super chic and sophisticated.
Can I do a French manicure at home?
Yes, you can! With a little practice and the right tools, doing a French manicure at home is totally doable. It can be a fun way to experiment and get creative with different designs and colors.
