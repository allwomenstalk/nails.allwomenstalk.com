# 7 Nail Polish Colors for the Holidays ...

Nail polish colors for the holidays are fun and festive and work for parties, church and any other event you plan to attend this year. You can’t go wrong with your classic pink colors, but the holidays are a fun time to experiment with other shades to give your entire look a new personality. Nail polish is available in a wide range of prices, so you can stock up on all these [nail polish colors](https://nails.allwomenstalk.com/unique-nail-colors-sure-to-get-attention/) for the holidays without breaking the bank. Check them out for your most memorable holiday yet.

## 1. Silver

![hair,woman,face,person,black,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mj/vc/sqfj9_hair_woman_face_person_black_600x908.jpg)

Silver is one of the best nail polish colors for the holidays. No matter which celebration you enjoy, silver is sure to give some sparkle to the occasion. Slick on silver polish for your office party or services on Christmas Eve. Silver comes in a range of shades, from very light to really dark and some even have glitter for even more festiveness this year.

## 2. Gold

![nail,finger,manicure,hand,nail care,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/h3/zp/zoyp8_nail_finger_manicure_hand_nail-care_600x600.jpg)

In much the same way, gold also brings elegance and richness to the holidays. You probably see gold decorations all over town and metallic clothing is pretty trendy when the holidays roll around. Paint some shiny gold polish on your fingers and toes and you’ll be all set for any celebration you plan to attend this year.

## 3. Bright Red

![eyebrow,face,hair,lip,cheek,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nf/dc/g82i7_eyebrow_face_hair_lip_cheek_600x800.jpg)

Nothing says Christmas like bright red. Am I right? That makes it a seasonal favorite that should definitely have a place in your rotation this year. I wear red all year round, but if you don’t, this holiday is the time to give it a try. Again, red comes in a huge variety of shades. Holly red and burgundy are two really great options for parties.

## 4. Sapphire

![nail,finger,blue,nail care,manicure,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mf/f7/bv3ji_nail_finger_blue_nail-care_manicure_600x800.jpg)

Jewel tones are great for the winter months. When the holidays arrive, bright blue nail polish looks fabulous. It pairs really well with all the other colors of the season. You might have noticed that blue and white are making a comeback as traditional Christmas colors, and they are classic Hanukkah colors too. That makes a rich blue color the perfect one to wear this holiday season.

## 5. Champagne

![nail,finger,nail care,nail polish,manicure,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ig/eu/lkug6_nail_finger_nail-care_nail-polish_manicure_600x600.jpg)

If you’re the kind of girl that wants something a bit more subtle, champagne is the color for you. After all, you break out the bubbly anytime you have something to celebrate, right? This shade is a subtle pink/tan color that tends to shine and sparkle when it catches the light. That makes it just right for any occasion.

## 6. White

![nail,finger,nail care,manicure,hand,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/lb/cs/g7pah_nail_finger_nail-care_manicure_hand_600x600.jpg)

Winter is white with all the snow that falls, so white is a wonderful nail polish choice. You can use it to paint the tips of your nails, then cover them in silver sparkles. Or you can paint your nails a dark color, then cover them with a coat of white to lighten things up. Your best bet for winter [white nails](https://nails.allwomenstalk.com/white-hot-nails-for-summertime/) is something with a bit of sheen so it isn’t too flat or boring.

## 7. Green

![nail,finger,green,nail care,yellow,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a7/xc/ngg69_nail_finger_green_nail-care_yellow_600x742.jpg)

If you’ve never worn green nail polish, now is the time to give it a try. Green is another classic holiday color, which makes it a great choice this year. There are so many shades of green on store shelves, so it can be daunting to make a choice. When you want a holiday worthy color, go for deep metallic greens that shine. Leave the lime green for summertime.

What is your favorite holiday nail polish color? I usually go for red or silver. Holiday nails are really fun so I hope you try one of these this year. Which one do you think will be your new favorite?
