# Keep Your Cuticles Clean with These Nail Products ...

Cuticles can be difficult to keep in good condition. Having your nails done, winter air ❄ 💨 and a variety of other factors can take their toll on them. These’re some great products you can use to keep your cuticles clean, neat and in good repair. When your cuticles look nice, the appearance of your entire hand changes.

## 1. Cuticle Stick

![Cuticle Stick](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ev/m1/pww3qirx566719d406050482952336_600x348.jpg)

A good cuticle stick is the first product you need to have well-kept cuticles. There’s not a certain brand that I’ve found to be better than others here so it’s completely up to you. Cuticles sticks are used to push your cuticles back so that your nail beds have a nice, even look. It’s easiest to do this right after a shower or soaking your hands. It can take a bit of practice to get the hang of this but you can do it.

## 2. CND Cuticle Eraser

![CND Cuticle Eraser](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dv/dx/p4i4oxi256671a04d549e406858742_500x479.jpg)

This’s an amazing little product that every woman needs as part of her manicure 💅routine. It can help you to erase the yucky cuticles that seem to be in the way. It works in two ways, exfoliating and moisturizing your cuticles. After using this, your cuticles will be more manageable and less noticeable. This makes a great gift to give to other nail enthusiasts, too.

## 3. OPI Manicure Scrub

![OPI Manicure Scrub](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/v3/ho/qrddyb5b56671a35a2c2c893557333_600x463.jpg)

Cuticle issues begin when dryness and roughness are an issue. This product by OPI addresses both of those. It’s a treatment for your full hand that your cuticles will benefit from, too. Enriched with olive oil, it’s the perfect product to soothe irritation away. It’s has a lovely scent to enjoy, too.

## 4. Essie Apricot Cuticle Oil

![Essie Apricot Cuticle Oil](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k4/w7/ud8me3un56671a6fb32c8584751034_600x568.jpg)

Sometimes you just feel like your cuticles need direct treatment. They’re dry, ragged and can even look embarrassing. This product is designed just for your cuticles, to give them the nourishment they need. It feels wonderful going on, just like you’d imagine a luscious nail treatment would feel. You can use it as a standalone treatment or as a part of your full manicure routine 👐.

## 5. CND Rescue Rx Daily Keratin Treatment

![CND Rescue Rx Daily Keratin Treatment](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/bn/cg/ljvtwgq056671ac11ed46544090854_548x535.jpg)

This’s a product I reach for regularly. It’s full of wonderful ingredients to nourish both your nails and cuticles. It’s an oily consistency that’s very moisturizing. You simply apply it and rub it in. It’s formulated with keratin to strengthen your nails while nourishing your cuticles at the same time. When your nails and cuticles are in need of recovery, reach for this product.

## 6. O’Keefe’s Working Hands

![O’Keefe’s Working Hands](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/eu/tg/w2r7q5ce56671af8edcc3310215626_600x527.jpg)

I use this product every single day of my life. It’s one of my beauty staples that I could not do without. For years, I had painful splits in the ends of my fingers that hurt terribly. Those became a thing of the past when I began to use this product before bed at night. Additionally, my cuticles softened up and became much less noticeable. It’s a product that’s a win all the way around.

## 7. EOS Hand Lotion

![EOS Hand Lotion](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xh/wj/amir97yd56671b3b0b205044695569_600x296.jpg)

While I love using O’Keefe’s Working Hands, I like something a little lighter to carry in my purse for use throughout the day. EOS hand lotion will keep your hands and cuticles in nice condition. It’s the perfect size to slip in your bag and such a cute design. I haven’t found an EOS product that I don’t love yet, have you? This one’s a keeper.

These’re 7 amazing products to give you clean, neat cuticles. What nail and cuticle care products do you love? I always read your tips!
