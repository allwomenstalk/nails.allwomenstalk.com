Frequently Asked Questions

What are gel nails?
Gel nails are a type of manicure that uses a special gel polish cured under UV or LED light for a long-lasting, glossy finish.
How long do gel nails last?
Gel nails typically last 2 to 3 weeks without chipping or peeling when properly applied and cared for.
Are gel nails damaging to natural nails?
When removed correctly, gel nails are generally safe, but frequent or improper removal can weaken natural nails.
Can I do gel nails at home?
Yes, with the right gel polish kit and UV/LED lamp, you can achieve salon-quality gel nails at home.
How do I remove gel nails safely?
Soak your nails in acetone or use professional gel polish removers to gently lift the gel without damaging your nails.
What are the best gel nail colors for women?
Classic shades like nude, red, and pink are timeless, but trendy colors like pastels and bold hues are also popular.
Can gel nails be applied on short nails?
Absolutely! Gel polish enhances both short and long nails, giving a clean and stylish look.
How do I maintain gel nails between salon visits?
Moisturize your cuticles regularly and avoid using your nails as tools to keep them looking fresh.
Is gel nail polish toxic?
Most gel polishes are safe when used as directed, but ensure good ventilation during application to avoid inhaling fumes.
Can gel nails cause allergic reactions?
Some people may experience sensitivity to gel products; always patch test if you have sensitive skin or allergies.
