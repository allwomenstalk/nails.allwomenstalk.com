Frequently Asked Questions

How can I prevent my nail polish from chipping quickly?
Apply a base coat and finish with a top coat to protect your polish and extend its wear.
What’s the best way to remove stubborn nail polish?
Use an acetone-based remover with a cotton pad and soak nails briefly for easier removal.
How do I fix smudged nail polish without starting over?
Quickly dip your nail in cold water to help the polish set and reduce smudging.
Can I use household items to remove glitter nail polish?
Yes, use foil wrapped around cotton soaked in remover to easily lift glitter polish.
How do I make my nail polish dry faster?
Apply thin coats and use a quick-dry top coat or a nail drying spray for speedy drying.
What’s a simple hack for perfectly shaped nails?
Use a coffee filter to gently buff your nails into shape without damaging them.
How can I avoid getting polish on my skin?
Apply petroleum jelly or a barrier cream around nails before painting to keep polish off skin.
What’s a trick for bold nail designs at home?
Use tape or stickers as stencils to create clean, professional-looking nail art.
How do I keep my nails healthy under polish?
Give your nails a break from polish occasionally and moisturize cuticles regularly.
Can I reuse old nail polish?
Add a few drops of nail polish thinner to revive thickened polish for easier application.
