# Mani Mania: Best Nude Polishes for Fall ...

Michelle Saunders, an Essie celebrity manicurist, has a lot to say about nude nail polishes! "They range in shade, tone, and depth," she said. "You want the polish to look like an extension of your hand. It looks really chic that way." It's all about looking at your nail beds - if you see pink, you should choose a rosy neutral. If you have beige nail beds, go for camel and sandy brown colors. Here's some of the best nude polishes for fall:
