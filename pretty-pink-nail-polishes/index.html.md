# 8 Pretty Pink Nail Polishes ...

A flirty, feminine shade of pink nail polish can take any spring or summer ensemble from boring to beautiful. Whether you’re in search of a tame hue you can wear to the office or a spicy shade you can rock during a night out, these eight pink nail polish picks will do the trick! From glimmering glitter to vibrant neon and everything in between, this lacquer roundup proves that your nails can be pretty in pink for any occasion.

## 1. L'Oreal Colour Riche Nail Hopeless Romantic Collection

![L'Oreal Colour Riche Nail Hopeless Romantic Collection](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nails/2013/04/1_loreal-colour-riche-nail-hopeless-romantic-collection_162x401.jpg)

No pink nail polish collection is complete without this charming shade. Doesn’t it remind you of Rapunzel’s dress from ‘Tangled’? Wear it with a flared cream skirt and lilac blouse to really feel like royalty. Buy this princess-worthy bottle now on Target.com for $4.99.

## 2. Neon Pink Nail Polish

![Neon Pink Nail Polish](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nails/2013/04/2_neon-pink-nail-polish_164x200.jpg)

Get your glow on with vibrant neon pink nails. Let your polish do the talkin’ by keeping the rest of your ensemble sleek and simple; a pleated leather skirt, strappy black wedges, and a silky white blouse should do the trick! Feeling particularly daring? Throw on a statement neon necklace –the chunkier, the better. Buy this beaming bottle now on Forever21.com for $2.80.

## 3. Deborah Lippmann Dream a Little Dream of Me

![Deborah Lippmann Dream a Little Dream of Me](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nails/2013/04/3_deborah-lippmann-dream-a-little-dream-of-me_96x205.jpg)

Described as “a cupcake frosting pink,” this scrumptious shade will give you a major sugar rush –minus the calories. Now that’s what I’d like to call a win-win! I’d wear it on my nails with a flirty white frock, adorable pink wedges, and loose curls. Yum! Scoop this shade up now on Barneys.com for $17.

## 4. ‘3 Free’ Nail Lacquer

![‘3 Free’ Nail Lacquer](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nails/2013/04/4_3-free-nail-lacquer_350x537.jpg)

If you’ve been scouring the ‘Net for a shade that won’t break office dress code, consider Butter London’s put-together pastel polish. Wear it with a sleek black pencil skirt and pastel yellow blouse to exude professionalism (and style!). Buy this bottle now on Nordstrom.com for $15.

## 5. Essie Navigate Her Spring Nail Polish Collection

![Essie Navigate Her Spring Nail Polish Collection](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nails/2013/04/5_essie-navigate-her-spring-nail-polish-collection_128x312.jpg)

There’s nothing quite like a shimmering fuchsia to add some spice to your warm weather wardrobe. Wear this standout shade with a flirty leather mini and a silky top for a night out with the girls. Or, take it on your next vacation and wear it as you lounge on the sand in your brand new bikini. Stunning! Scoop this sassy hue up now on Zappos.com for $8.

## 6. Anna Sui Nail Art Nail Polish

![Anna Sui Nail Art Nail Polish](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nails/2013/04/6_anna-sui-nail-art-nail-polish_137x190.jpg)

This gorgeous, glittering lacquer resides in an equally striking dress bottle. I’d wear it with a little black dress and pink peep-toe pumps on a dinner date. Buy this polish now on UrbanOutfitters.com for $15 (tip: this bottle was made to be shown off on your dresser!).

## 7. Deborah Lippmann Candy Shop

![Deborah Lippmann Candy Shop](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nails/2013/04/7_deborah-lippmann-candy-shop_104x205.jpg)

If you’re the type of gal who has a closet that’s chock full of pink garb and accessories? Then this polish will give you another dose of pink, but with a rainbow of other colors thrown into the glittery mix. Wear it to a street fair or festival with cutoff denim shorts, a flowing top, and a floppy hat. Buy this candy-colored polish now on Barneys.com for $19.

## 8. Ciaté ‘Cupcake Queen’ Paint Pot

![Ciaté ‘Cupcake Queen’ Paint Pot](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nails/2013/04/8_ciat-cupcake-queen-paint-pot_350x537.jpg)

This lovely, vibrant pink shade will add flair to your going-out wardrobe. Wear it with a pleated mini and a denim button front top on a Saturday afternoon shopping trip with the girls. Pick this polish up now on Nordstrom.com for $15.
