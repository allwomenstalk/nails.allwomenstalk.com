# 7 Trendy Nail Art Tutorials That Are Easy to Copy ...

After watching countless **nail art tutorials**, I've memorized all of the trendy designs and colors by heart. Doing my nails myself has become a form of therapy for me in the sense that it is usually the only time I have where I can just sit back and relax. I've been trying out more complex styles and am incredibly pleased with the results I've gotten so far. These amazing nail art tutorials are so easy to copy that it won't be long before your fellow fashionistas crown you as the next nail guru!

## 1. Bright and Bold Nail Art

One of my favorite aspects of visiting the channel of YouTube sensation "MissJenFABULOUS" is the amount of nail art tutorials that she effectively combines into one video. The fact that they are always easy to follow makes this even better. I love rocking the second look whenever I go out with my friends. The black and silver bring out the shine in that gorgeous base color.

## 2. Water Spotted Nail Art

As my first time trying out this next nail design, I have to say that the end product came out really well. I was nervous that the process would be exceedingly tedious; however, it was actually pretty simple! YouTube user "cutepolish" managed to explain it in a true-to-life manner.

## 3. Burberry Nail Art

Am I the only one drooling over this Burberry inspired manicure? YouTube nail artist "NailsByiMichelley" perfectly recreated the signature Burberry print. While the manicure took a bit of time to get it right, I was definitely pleased with the final product of my labor. When it comes to Burberry, how much more chic can you get?

## 4. Colorful Leopard Nails

I am a major fan of nail designs that grab your attention. YouTube nail guru "iMarlene25" does exactly that in her tutorial for this brightly colored manicure. Leopard print nails will always be my go-to design; I love that she adds this bold twist to the typical animal print nail art by incorporating a variety of colors and shapes.

## 5. Zebra Nail Art

Speaking of animal, those hot pink tips are fabulous! I love how each color comes together for an incredibly sharp look. I would have never thought of making a French manicure this fierce. YouTube nail artist "prettypolishes" is absolutely brilliant and creative; I don't think I'll ever stop gushing over this bold manicure.

## 6. Textured Chevron Nail Art

Chevron nail art is becoming increasingly popular. Although I am not sure how the trend started, I am more than okay with it! YouTube star "elleandish // Janelle" experimented with quite a few interesting pieces (nail studs, zigzag scissors, etc) in order to achieve a textured look. I'm really impressed with how this turned out! I honestly didn't expect to fall in love with the diverse textures incorporated into this nail art.

## 7. Pink Plaid Nail Art

Last but not least, this just may be my favorite look to try when getting ready for date night. The best part about this look is that it is really simple to replicate. Am I the only one who thinks that this manicure totally screams Avril Lavigne?

I will never get tired of testing different nail polish looks. What trends are you itching to try? When it comes to manicures for date night, what are your go-to colors?
