21 Nails Colors You Should Nab for Spring ...

Formula X #ColorCurators: Cupcakes and Cashmere Edition in Latte Run • Formula X #ColorCurators: Patrick Starrr Edition in She Better Werk • Marc Jacobs Beauty Enamored Hi-Shine Nail Polish in My Peeps • Dior Vernis Gel Shine and Long Wear Nail Lacquer in Wild about Pink • Ciaté London Mini Paint Pot Nail Polish in Lucky 7 • More ...

By Jennifer

The sun's shining, the birds are singing, the flowers are blooming, and the Spring 2016 nail colors are here! Let's all choose our favorite new hue to welcome the warmer weather, shall we?

1. Formula X #ColorCurators: Cupcakes and Cashmere Edition in Latte Run

Price: $12.50 at sephora.com
Nude is here to stay for Spring 2016, especially if it's this one, a collaboration between Formula X and lifestyle blogger Emily Schuman of Cupcakes and Cashmere.

2. Formula X #ColorCurators: Patrick Starrr Edition in She Better Werk

Price: $12.50 at sephora.com
This soft peony pink is another color collaboration, this time with vlogger Patrick Starrr. The other hues in this series are also pink, one a hot pink glitter and the other a fuschia.

3. Marc Jacobs Beauty Enamored Hi-Shine Nail Polish in My Peeps

Price: $18 at sephora.com
I'm just going to go ahead and assume this shade is named after everyone's favorite marshmallow candy treat. Yes? Yes.

4. Dior Vernis Gel Shine and Long Wear Nail Lacquer in Wild about Pink

Price: $27 at sephora.com
This limited edition color is so pretty, once you swipe it on, you'll be wild about pink, too.

5. Ciaté London Mini Paint Pot Nail Polish in Lucky 7

Price: $9 at sephora.com
This juicy orange color is perfect for spring! If you're not sure whether you're like it (of course you will!), the mini pot is great.

6. BURBERRY Nail Polish in Sage Green

Price: $22 at sephora.com
This spring-y green is only available online, so if you see it in stock, add it your cart, tout de suite!

7. DOLCE&GABBANA the Nail Lacquer in

Price: $27 at sephora.com
Who says grey is boring and bland? This shade is exotic, somehow tropical. I love it!

8. NAILS INC. NAILKALE in Victoria

Price: $14 at sephora.com
This luxe, deep red may seem more like a fall or winter hue, but it's so gorgeous, don't retire it yet!

9. Formula X Divine Stardust in Hera

Price: $12.50 at sephora.com
This shade is so divine, it's named after the queen of goddesses herself.

10. Christian Louboutin Nail Colour in Just Nothing

Price: $50 at sephora.com
This sandy nude is on the warmer side, a nude that's all sunshine and ideal for spring.

11. NARS Nail Polish in Orgasm

Price: $20 at sephora.com
It's pinky peach with a subtle gold sheen, a shade you can layer for a more dramatic look.

12. Deborah Lippmann Nail Lacquer in Break 4 Love

Price: $18 at sephora.com
I'm starting to see a trend, and that trend is very pretty in pink! This color is no exception - so ladylike and lovely!

13. Formula X #ColorCurators: Song of Style Edition in Mintfluencer

Price: $12.50 at sephora.com
This color is the exact shade of my favorite flavor if ice cream, mint chocolate chip. It's a color collaboration between Formula X and fashion blogger Aimee Song of Song of Style.

14. KVD VS Formula X Nail Polish in Vampira

Price: $12.50 at sephora.com
This color collaboration between Formula X and bad girl Kat Von D is fab - the color matches the KVD Studded Kiss lip color of the same name. Love!

15. JINsoon Operetta Collection in Cantata

Price: $18 at sephora.com
Oh, how lovely! It's a gorgeous shade of shimmering silver with a hint of purple, inspired by the incredible opera costumes designed by Erté.

16. JINsoon X Tila March Nail Polish Collection in Enflammee

Price: $18 at sephora.com
This color? It's a skip right past spring and into hot, hot summer, a color collaboration between JINsoon and designer Tamara Taichman of Tila March.

17. Olivia Palermo X Ciaté London Nail Collection in My off Duty Nude

Price: $24 at sephora.com
Yet another gorgeous color collaboration, this one with Ciaté London and the glamorous it-girl Olivia Palermo. This pink is just so soft and delicate!

18. Deborah Lippmann Roar Collection in Respect

Price: $18 at sephora.com
Inspired by girl power (yeah!), this collection features bold, bright colors, and the most spring-ready is this one, in the red family.

19. NAILS INC. Alice + Olivia by Stacey Bendet Nail Collection

Price: $15 at sephora.com
This is just a spectacular color, the opposite of a neutral, but still something you can wear with anything in your spring wardrobe.

20. Tenoverten Nail Polish in Prince

Price: $18 at sephora.com
Nothing says "spring" like lilacs, the scent and the color. That's why I just had to include this polish - the color is exactly the shade of my grandmother's lilacs.

21. Formula X the Colors in Luscious

Price: $5.25 at sephora.com
Grass green. Is there any better color for spring? I think not.
