# Best 🆒🆕 Nail Colors for Fall for Girls Who Love a Fresh Mani ...

It's fall! Time to put away the pale pinks and other pastels and hunt for the best new [nail colors](https://nails.allwomenstalk.com/unique-nail-colors-sure-to-get-attention/) for fall ... and your favorite jeans and sweaters, of course. Here are all of the hottest hues for fall 2016, ready for wearing right now.

## 1. Aubergine 🍆

![pink, nail care, magenta, cosmetics, lip,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/xt/hl/wi8xyuct57d01ded9a998458380579_600x583.jpg)

Price: $10.50 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
While the aubergine emoji looks a little obscene, this color is divine, and it's ultra-hot this fall. I love this Formula X take on the hue; I wonder where I can find a matching lippie?

## 2. Lilac

![NARS, color, pink, nail care, lilac,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wv/mw/n38y6woy57d01dec77d4e560851521_600x582.jpg)

Price: $20 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This may seem like a pastel-y spring or summer color, but it's also fab for fall, so if you have a bottle you've fallen in love with, wear it til it snows!

## 3. Navy

![cobalt blue, multimedia, cosmetics, nail care, magenta,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cf/vz/w44zwst857d01ded8782c517055042_600x583.jpg)

Price: $18 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
I've been seeing this deep navy blue hue everywhere this season, and I love it! It goes so well with almost everything in our fall wardrobes, including our darkest-wash skinny jeans.

## 4. Grey

![Dior, perfume, product, skin, cosmetics,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rf/rr/cumdl3ns57d01ded46a1c386890805_600x582.jpg)

Price: $27 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This is another hold-over from seasons past (think spring 2016), but it's still gorgeous and it's something you can still wear until spring rolls around again, your multi-season neutral.

## 5. Red, Red Wine 🍷

![Butter London, nail, nail care, cosmetics, skin,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ew/xx/s7d980bd57d01dee134f5205151020_600x536.jpg)

Price: $18 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This isn't really a new hue, more of an update to a vampy classic. Wear this from now til Christmas, and maybe even til the bulbs you planted this fall bloom in the spring.

## 6. Marvelous Mint

![NARS, aqua, turquoise, nail, nail care,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/de/wt/nxqyh2wv57d01ded0d2b3992790289_600x583.jpg)

Price: $20 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
There's another green up next, but this one's listed first because I just love it so much... I love it with crisp whites and charcoal greys -- think fall officewear -- don't you?

## 7. Evergreen 🌲

![clothing, perfume, skin, cosmetics, powder,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ph/ca/lcbno1fl57d01df20ece7235247920_600x578.jpg)

Price: $18 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Marc Jacobs calls this color "charcoal green," and it works. The hue is truly more grey, with just a hint of the color you'll be seeing on cardis all season long.
