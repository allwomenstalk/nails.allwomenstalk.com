16 Mermaid

Source: Easy Fall Fashion Trends 2015/2016
Tell me this doesn't remind you of a mermaid toes!

***

Summer is the perfect time to show off your pedicure. With so many fun and creative ideas out there, you can find the perfect look to make your feet stand out. One of the hottest trends for summer is the Mermaid Pedicure. This look is inspired by the beautiful and mysterious mermaids of the sea.

The Mermaid Pedicure starts with a base color of aqua or turquoise. This color is then accentuated with a light shimmer of silver or gold. To complete the look, the toes are decorated with seashells, starfish, and other ocean-inspired designs. You can also add a bit of sparkle with rhinestones or glitter.

To make sure your Mermaid Pedicure stays looking its best, use a good quality base coat and top coat. This will help protect your nails from chipping and fading. It's also important to use a non-toxic nail polish remover to avoid any potential damage to your nails.
