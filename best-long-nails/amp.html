Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best long nail shapes for everyday wear?
Oval and almond shapes are great for everyday wear as they are stylish yet less prone to breakage.
How can I make my long nails stronger?
Keep your nails moisturized, avoid harsh chemicals, and consider using a strengthening base coat.
Are long nails harder to maintain?
Long nails require regular care and gentle handling, but with the right routine, they can be easy to maintain.
Can I wear long nails if I type a lot?
Yes, but choosing shapes like oval or rounded can make typing more comfortable with long nails.
What nail polish colors look best on long nails?
Bold colors and intricate nail art often look stunning on long nails, but nude shades are also classy and versatile.
How often should I get my long nails professionally done?
Every 2-3 weeks is ideal to keep your long nails looking fresh and to prevent damage.
Are long acrylic nails better than natural long nails?
Acrylics add durability and design options, but natural nails are healthier with proper care.
What foods help promote nail growth?
Eating foods rich in biotin, vitamins E and B, and protein supports healthy nail growth.
Can long nails cause any health issues?
If not kept clean, long nails can harbor bacteria, so hygiene is very important.
What's the best way to remove long nail polish at home?
Use a gentle, acetone-free remover and soak nails briefly to avoid damage.
