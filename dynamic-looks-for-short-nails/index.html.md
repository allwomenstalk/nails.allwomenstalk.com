# 34 Dynamic Looks for Short Nails ...

There are so many fantastic looks for short nails that you don't need to fear getting a manicure just because you don't have long nails. It doesn't matter if you're trying to stop biting, if you need to have short nails for practical reasons, or if you simply like them that way. You can still go crazy with polish, and either highlight your [short nails](https://nails.allwomenstalk.com/nail-art-inspos-for-girls-wanting-to-class-up-their-short-nails/) or make them look a little longer. You're sure to find some great looks for short nails here, so get all your manicure tools ready!

## 1. Neutral and Accented

![nail,finger,nail care,nail polish,manicure,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/uy/fx/w7h9o_nail_finger_nail-care_nail-polish_manicure_600x800.jpg)

Via [chellogorgeous.com](https://goo.gl/agkMLt)
Neutral nail colors create some of the best looks for short nails, so don't shy away from a shade just because you think it's boring. Wow with an accent nail instead, because sometimes all you need is a little glitter.

## 2. Variations on the French Tip

_ Via myfashioncents.com_
Here's yet another variation on the French tip that lends itself well to shorter fingernails. Go under the edge of your nail, keep everything else clear and glossy, and once again, embrace the glitter!

## 3. Polka Dot Tips

_ Via trendsfever.com_
As you'll see, French tips and all the variations thereof really work with short nails. Here, the white makes the shape stand out, while the innovative tips lengthen and pop.
