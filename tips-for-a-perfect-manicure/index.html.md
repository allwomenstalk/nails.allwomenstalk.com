# 7 Tips for a Perfect Manicure ...

A **perfect manicure** is like the cherry on top of a polished look. It can boost your confidence and show off your personality - all without saying a word. Trust me, as someone who managed to paint polka dots on my nails while balancing a precarious bowl of Ramen during my university days, I know how impactful a well-done manicure can be. In this article, I'm going to share with you seven game-changing tips to achieve that salon-quality look right from the comfort of your own home - and in the exciting year of 2024, when self-care is all the rage!

First up, let's tackle the big question: why go for a **DIY manicure**? Well, besides the obvious perk of saving some hard-earned cash (hello, student loans!), a DIY manicure can also be a fun and therapeutic process. Personalization is paramount - you can choose your favorite colors, patterns, and even add a sprinkle of glitter without anyone batting an eyelid. Plus, achieving that **flawless nail art** by yourself feels incredibly rewarding. Just imagine the compliments rolling in when you tell your colleagues or classmates that you did it all by yourself.

Now, let’s delve into the juicy stuff: the essential tips. First and foremost, it's important to focus on the basics, like **nail care**. Healthy nails are the foundation of any manicure, so you need to start by keeping them clean, well-moisturized, and in good shape. Have you ever heard about the wonders of cuticle oil? If not, it’s time we spill the tea. Cuticle oil is like the secret fairy dust for your nails, ensuring they stay hydrated and strong. For more insights, drop by our section on [{nail care fundamentals}](#point2).

One of the more humorous but vital tips – invest in a good nail file. It does make a difference! While it might be tempting to borrow your roommate’s suspiciously dull emery board (I’ve been there, don’t judge), a quality nail file can save you from many a heartache. Trust me, I’ve learned this the hard way. For the uninitiated, I’ll discuss the ins and outs of this must-have tool in the segment on [{choosing the right tools}](#point4).

Moving on, let’s not forget the art of polishing. A good, long-lasting **nail polish** is every manicure enthusiast's secret weapon. But how do you choose the best one from an endless aisle of colorful little bottles? Sometimes it feels like a battle of gladiators in the beauty aisle. We'll cover all the bases on this in our section dedicated to [{best nail polish brands in 2024}](#point5).

So buckle up, fellow nail aficionados! Ready your cuticle oils, fetch that high-tech nail dryer, and dive into these seven tips to transform your manicure game. Whether you're a newbie or a seasoned nail artist, you'll find something illuminating (and hopefully a bit entertaining) to elevate your next manicure session.

## 1. Prep!

One of those all-important tips for a perfect manicure, prepping isn’t something you’ll want to skip if you want a salon-worthy mani! Seriously, imagine that gorgeous, attention-worthy new shade surrounded by cuticles and hangnails gone wild! Not the best picture, I’m sure you’ll agree! Nails are not the only ones to deserve pampering, which is why you should reserve some of that good stuff for the surrounding skin too, and use a cuticle softening method of choice to ensure a painless cleanup. Push cuticles back carefully, then gently buff the part of the nail they used to cover to remove any bits of skin and smooth out nail surface. Hangnails come next – they should be soft by now, allowing you to come in with your scissors and snip them off easily! Voila! All done!

## 2. Don’t Forget to Shape!

No mani will look awesome without awesomely shaped nails, thus this tip for a perfect manicure, of course! Grab a nail file, then, and start working your way to a perfect-looking nail! I like to file the sides of my nails first to ensure a clean, symmetric, elongated look and remove any remaining traces of hangnails (and this is actually a much better alternative to more cutting), then either square the top or, if I’ve cut my nails short, file along the curve to make sure the line is completely smooth.

## 3. Good Base Saves the Day

A great base is a great choice – don’t forget that! It smooths and perfects the nail surface, making the application much easier and the finished look much better, even when it comes to foily, unforgiving formulas. It also helps the nail color adhere much better, turning even the easiest to chip polishes into something you can wear much longer, plus it keeps your nails stain-free (ideal for dark polishes) and super strong!

## 4. Paint like a Pro

This well-known manicure trick could save you a lot of brushstrokes, so keep notes and make sure you give it a test run the next time you decide to paint your nails. And with that said, here’s the big secret – three strokes! Place the brush parallel with the cuticle and as close as possible to it then pull to get a nice straight (well, as straight as possible) line along the center of your nail. One brush stroke for each side and voila – you’ve coated your entire nail in just three easy strokes! What this method does is help you get the polish on perfectly every time without that annoying pooling at the sides that can occur with runnier polishes. Not to mention that, when used with a brush that fans out nicely, this trick could help you coat a whole nail in one stroke! Awesome, isn’t it?

## 5. Clean up

Cleaning up your work definitely is one of those manicure tricks that make a pro job look…well…like a pro job! Luckily, we’re not talking rocket science here, which means that you can get that pro-looking manicure at home with no extra cost! Use an old makeup brush, a makeup brush you found too stiff to use for your face or even a small painting brush – it can be slanted or oval like a foundation brush and it will help you clean your cuticles and the sides of your nails like a true pro! Ever wondered how those makeup bloggers manage to have that perfectly shaped gap between their cuticles and nail polish? Well, this is how! Dip the brush into your nail polish remover and use smooth but firm moves to clean up your cuticle line as well as all stains (if any). It might take some getting used to but the results are so, so, so worth it.

## 6. Cheat

Ready for one tip for a perfect manicure that is sure to make you go, “Yay”? Well, here’s it is plain and simple – cheat! You’ve got special effect toppers, magnetic nail polishes, French manicure strips to make the application easy and perfect-looking even if you’ve never done it before, foils…you name it! The beauty industry of today has literally taken it all, put a big bow on it and handed it to you on a silver platter! All you have to do is to reach out and take it! Why not? It does mean having a bit more products than you’re used to but gives you the “WOW” appearance even if you’re not paying big bucks to get your nails professionally groomed!

## 7. Top off

You’re almost there! All that’s left for you to do is apply a top coat! Skip this step if you’re using a special effect topper of course but keep it in mind if you’re hoping to score a long-wear DIY mani! You can get a 2 in 1 base and top coat and save yourself the trouble of having two different products or opt for some of the many interesting clear top coats pretty much every brand has to offer. Apply it, wait for it to dry then wash your hands, gently pat them dry and apply a cuticle oil of choice! Voila! You’ve just mastered all the manicure tricks you’ll ever need in order to give yourself a seriously gorgeous looking manicure!
