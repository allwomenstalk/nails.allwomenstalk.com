11 Dry, Drag Marble

Source: A Girl and Her Polish
This technique is so easy and it gives you a great looking abstract dragon when you're finished.

***

This technique, called "Dry, Drag Marble," is a great way to create an abstract dragon design. It is an easy technique to do and requires no special tools or materials. All that is needed is a light colored base color, a few darker colors, and a toothpick. To create the design, the toothpick is used to drag the darker colors through the base color to create the dragon design. This look can be further enhanced by adding gems, crystals, or other embellishments. This design is perfect for anyone who loves dragons and wants to show off their unique style.
