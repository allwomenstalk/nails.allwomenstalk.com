Frequently Asked Questions

What are the latest fashion nail trends?
Current trends include minimalist designs, pastel colors, and geometric patterns.
How can I make my nails look fashionable at home?
Try simple designs like French tips or use nail stickers and stamps for a quick, chic look.
What nail shapes are popular right now?
Almond, coffin, and oval shapes are very trendy and flattering.
How do I care for my nails to keep them healthy?
Moisturize your cuticles regularly and avoid biting or using nails as tools.
Can I use gel polish for long-lasting fashion nails?
Yes, gel polish is great for durability and maintaining vibrant colors for weeks.
What colors are best for a stylish look?
Neutral tones, soft pinks, nudes, and bold reds work well for various occasions.
How often should I get a manicure to keep nails looking fresh?
Every 2-3 weeks is ideal to maintain nail health and style.
Are there any easy nail art ideas for beginners?
Yes, try polka dots, stripes, or gradient colors using simple tools like dotting pens or brushes.
Can nail accessories like gems and stickers damage nails?

How do I choose the best nail polish for my skin tone?
Warmer skin tones look great with coral and browns, while cooler tones suit blues and purples.
