# 7 Nail Art Vloggers You Should Check out ...

Nail lovers, it's time to get serious: If you have ever found yourself on the verge of panicking over your chipped nails, you may want to take a seat because these fabulous nail art vloggers have exactly what you need. Because of my major obsession with DIY projects, I love spending a lot of time surfing through different YouTube channels whenever I'm in need of the perfect look for any occasion. One of my favorite purchases is nail products because of the fact that they are the easiest way to make a plain outfit pop! If you ever feel as if you are in a fashion rut, you should definitely out these incredibly talented nail art vloggers.

## 1. Michelle Phan

Let's be honest; would any list featuring some of the best nail art vloggers out there be complete without an appearance by the lovely Michelle Phan? Not only is she known for her helpful beauty video guides, she also dishes out really awesome tips on how to get the perfect manicure. Can you seriously believe how amazing this tutorial on the water lily design is?

## 2. Elleandish // Janelle

I never get tired of talking about how beautiful elleandish // Janelle's nail tutorials always manage to turn out! She's actually one of the reasons I became so obsessed with DIY manicures; I never thought that it would be so easy to replicate some of the hottest looks without the pricey cost. Her video on painting nails using your dominant and non-dominant hand definitely turned me into a nail art monster. I don't think I'll ever get over her pink & orange Kate Spade inspired nails.

## 3. IndigoNova1

If you're new to the nail art trend and you are afraid that you won't be able to be creative, IndigoNova1's channel is a great place to start. I can honestly say that she explains her videos so well that there is no chance that you won't be able to catch on and achieve the look you want, beginner or not.

## 4. MissJenFABULOUS

Forget the nail salon; why spend a lot of money on professional manicures when you can get the look for less right at home? MissJenFABULOUS is certainly no newbie when it comes to the nail art game; I just love that her tutorials are always straight to the point.

## 5. Cutepolish

Frozen lovers, you've got to admit that this one is pretty cool. If you don't think you'll ever be ready to let go of Frozen, you may want to test this one out a few times! There's a reason why vlogger cutepolish has a huge social following; she is great at taking requests from fans and turning them into masterpieces.

## 6. Robin Moses Nail Art

The level of intricacy in Robin Moses Nail Art's tutorials makes it hard not to fall in love with her work. If you love flowers as much as I do, you'll be glad to know that she is one of the best nail art geniuses when it comes to floral designs.

## 7. Meliney

Lastly, Meliney's tutorials taught me how to recreate the oh so flawless french manicure and water marble nail art. The fact that she always lists the tools used in every tutorial also doesn't hurt. She keeps her instructions simple, cool and classy.

You can never have enough options to explore when it comes to having fresh and fabulous nails! What are some other nail art channels that you guys love to follow? Do you have any go-to designs?
