1 Put Your Nail Polish in the Fridge for 15 Min before Applying

***

When it comes to nail polish, there are many hacks that can help make your manicure last longer and look better. One of the most important tips is to put your nail polish in the fridge for 15 minutes before applying. This helps the polish to become thicker and less likely to chip or peel. It also helps the polish to dry faster and more evenly.

Another tip is to always use a base coat before applying the color. This helps to protect your nails from staining and also helps the color to last longer. It is also important to apply thin coats of polish rather than one thick coat. This helps the polish to dry faster and prevents bubbling.

When you are done with your manicure, it is important to apply a top coat to seal the color and help it last even longer. A quick-drying top coat is best as it helps the color to set quickly and prevents smudging.

2 Give Your Nails a Marble Effect by Swirling Two Polishes Together and Rolling Your Nail over the Edge of the Spoon

***

Marble effect nails are an easy way to add a unique touch to your manicure. All you need is two different nail polishes and a spoon. To create the marble effect, swirl the two colors together and then roll your nail over the edge of the spoon. The result is a beautiful, marbled pattern that’s sure to turn heads.

Achieving the perfect marble effect can take a bit of practice, but once you’ve mastered the technique, you’ll be able to create a variety of stunning manicures. You can also customize your marble effect by using different colors, or by adding glitter or metallic accents.

When creating a marble effect, it’s important to use a non-porous surface, such as a spoon or a piece of plastic wrap. This will ensure that the polish doesn’t stick to the surface and ruin your design. It’s also important to work quickly, as the polish will dry quickly and make it difficult to achieve the desired effect.

5 A Dry Quick Tip

***

If you're in a rush and don't have time for a full manicure to dry, there's a simple solution. Dunk your freshly painted nails into a bowl of ice-cold water for about a minute. The cold water helps to set the polish faster, allowing you to move on with your day without the fear of smudges. It's crucial, though, to gently pat them dry afterward to avoid ruining your newly applied color. This trick is a lifesaver when you're already running late but still want your nails to look impeccably done!

7 Make a Gel Manicure Last Longer

***

A gel manicure is a great way to get long-lasting and vibrant nail color. The gel polish is applied to the nail in thin layers and then cured under a UV or LED light.
