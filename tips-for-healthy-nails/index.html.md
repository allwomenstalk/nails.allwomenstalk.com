# 5 Awesome Tips for Beautiful and Healthy Nails ...

Do you need some **tips for healthy nails**? Every woman already knows the importance of clean and healthy nails; our nails are the mirrors of our hygiene and we should always do our best at keeping them as healthy as possible. Many people all around the world struggle with weak and very messy nails, but why? Want to know how to prevent your nails from failing so much? We bring you 5 awesome **tips for healthy nails**.

## 1. Lemon and Honey

Take two spoons of honey and add 4-5 drops of lemon; make sure you mix it up so it is all even. When the mixture is done, massage the mixture all over your nails and let it do the job for 20-30 minutes. Wash your hands afterward and use a moisturizing cream for a good finish. This is one of the best **tips for healthy nails**.

## 2. Olive Oil

Put your hands in warm olive oil and hold them there for 15-20 minutes. After that, massage your nails and the skin around them with very gentle and careful moves.

## 3. Vitamin E

If you are one of those people who suffer from very weak nails, there is a high chance your body is missing vitamin E. You can solve this issue by buying vitamin E oil and massaging it into your nails. The results will be visible after a couple of days. In case you do not have the oil, just start eating foods that are rich in vitamin E, such as almonds, spinach, chard, kale, turnip and a lot more!

## 4. Lemon and Olive Oil

Squeeze an entire half of the lemon into 2 teaspoons of olive oil. Mix it well and then hold your nails in the mixture for 10 minutes. Wash it all off and repeat the process every three days.

## 5. Vaseline and Olive Oil

Mix up the Vaseline with a couple of drops of olive oil and gently scrub it all over your nails and especially on the skin around your nails. Let it sit for 5 minutes and then wash it off. The results will be visible in less than a week. Repeat this process three days a week and see how beautiful your nails can actually be!
