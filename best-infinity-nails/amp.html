Frequently Asked Questions

What are infinity nails?
Infinity nails feature long, elegant designs often with shimmering or holographic effects for a standout look.
How long do infinity nails typically last?
With proper care, infinity nails can last 2 to 3 weeks before needing a refill or touch-up.
Are infinity nails safe for natural nails?
Yes, when applied and removed correctly by a professional, they should not damage your natural nails.
Can I do infinity nails at home?
While possible, it's best to have infinity nails done by a skilled nail technician to achieve the best results.
What styles work best with infinity nails?
Glitter, chrome, ombre, and jewel accents complement the sleek, futuristic vibe of infinity nails.
How do I care for my infinity nails?
Moisturize your cuticles regularly and avoid harsh chemicals to keep your nails looking fresh.
Can I customize infinity nails with colors?
Absolutely! Infinity nails come in many colors or even transparent effects to match your style.
What occasions are infinity nails best for?
They're perfect for parties, special events, or whenever you want to add a bold touch to your look.
Are infinity nails expensive?
Prices vary by salon, but they may cost a bit more due to the intricate designs and materials used.
How do I remove infinity nails safely?
Have them professionally removed to avoid nail damage and maintain nail health.
