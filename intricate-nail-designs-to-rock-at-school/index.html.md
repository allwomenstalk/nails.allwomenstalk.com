# 7 Intricate Nail Designs to Rock at School ...

I've always found detailed and intricate nail designs to be quite interesting. From the bold colors to the carefully dotted lines, nail art has taken off. The best part about this is that it has become easier to perfect the look of a fine manicure without having to spend a lot of money to do so. These intricate nail designs will make you the envy of all your classmates.

## 1. Lightning Bolt Nail Art

This fabulous nail art tutorial by "elleandish // Janelle" immediately caught my attention as I was searching for creative and intricate nail designs. As I'm always up for trying new things, I decided to give this a try and fell in love with it by the time I finished only one hand. I really like the sharp contrast between the gold glitter polish and the black nail polish. You will certainly be the talk of the town while flaunting this sweet manicure.

## 2. Chevron Accent Nail Art

Accent nails are become a huge hit amongst nail art lovers. This design by "Polishpedia" is no exception. The mint nail polish used in this tutorial is absolutely striking and complements the chevron pattern very well. The contrasting colors add the perfect touch to this already beautiful look. It's easy to see why the color mint is one of the biggest trends on the runway right now.

## 3. Biker Studded Nail Art

I love how fierce and edgy this next look by "SuperWOWstyle" is. The finely crafted zippers and studs are perfect for those days when you want to make a statement. When I first stumbled upon this tutorial, I thought that it was going to be incredibly difficult to replicate. However, this nail guru explains it in a way that anyone can try it. How could you not be head over heels for this crafty design? I could totally see Lady Gaga testing this out.

## 4. Newspaper Print Nail Art

As a writer, my inner nerd completely freaked out over this next design. What could be cooler than rocking newspaper print nails? I've always wondered how to recreate this look; the fact that "luxuriousnails" makes it look so easy is even better! In order to copy this look with precision, make sure to use 70% rubbing alcohol.

## 5. Aztec Nail Art

While seemingly complex in nature, this nail art by Emma Barton is actually simple to duplicate! Aztec print has become increasingly popular over the last few years because of its bold and bright appearance. I love how all of the colors come together.

## 6. Sexy Stiletto with Red Bottom Nail Art

Fashionastas will definitely appreciate this tutorial. Inspired by the famous Christian Louboutin stiletto, I love that YouTube star "sewcrafty1" expertly replicates the red bottom. The greatest aspect of this manicure is that it's subtle enough to rock at school while also being fierce enough for date night.

## 7. Galaxy Nail Art

This final look by "MissJenFABULOUS" is simple to achieve yet just as amazing as the other nail designs. With its mixture of dark hues, this galaxy manicure won't need much to amaze bystanders. I love that this nail art basically speaks for itself.

All of these nail designs are out of this world! What are your favorite designs to wear to school? What colors stand out the most to you?
