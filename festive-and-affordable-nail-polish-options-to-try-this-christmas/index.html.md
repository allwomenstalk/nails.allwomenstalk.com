# 7 Festive and Affordable Nail Polish Options to Try This Christmas ...

Ladies, we love finding deals and reasonable prices on everything, including affordable nail polish. At this time of year we spend loads of time and money making other people happy. When we come across great prices for things we love (and a few things we didn’t know that we loved, ha!), it is fantastic! A couple things that make me very happy are pretty colors and great deals. We have seven suggestions for festive and affordable nail polish options that offer both to try this Christmas.

## 1. Maybelline Color Show in Paint the Town

![Maybelline Color Show in Paint the Town](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/20193848_3926_409x386.jpg)

We begin our short list of affordable nail polish options to try this Christmas with a very familiar color. Paint the Town Red by Maybellie Color Show is a cute shade of red. The tint is bright and cheerful! It also reminds me of candy canes and Santa Claus’ Christmas suit. Plus you can get it for just $2.97.

## 2. Revlon Nail Enamel in Pure Pearl

![Revlon Nail Enamel in Pure Pearl](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/20193848_2462_499x498.jpg)

If the Maybelline’s Paint The Town reminds me of Santa’s suit then Pure Pearl by Revlon Nail Enamel is the suit’s white trim and pom-pom. The pearl finish looks like satin and reminds me of Christmas movies and hot chocolate! (I don’t know why, but it does.) If you like nail polish with a pearl finish then give this one a try for the holidays. It's yours for just $3.97.

## 3. KleanColor in Leaves Jingle

![KleanColor in Leaves Jingle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/20193848_7169_499x351.jpg)

All this talk about Santa’s suit makes me think of elf stockings and Christmas trees! (Can you tell I’m excited about the season?) The Leaves Jingle polish by KleanColor is a great shade of green. The color is rich and iridescent without being overwhelming. It even has tiny specks of glitter that highlight the color and complement the sheen. If you are daring enough, try it! I know I will, especially since it's under $3.00.

## 4. Wet N Wild Fergie in Gold Album

![Wet N Wild Fergie in Gold Album](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/20193848_3362_449x426.jpg)

Gold nail polish is a bold choice, especially for the holidays. The variety of gold nail polish colors available is pretty large. It can be a little challenging to find the right shade for your event and skin tone. Gold Album from the Wet n Wild Fergie collection is a nicely muted, yet shimmery, gold. This gold reminds me of Christmas lights and tree toppers. Pick it up for just $3.49.

## 5. Hard Candy Crystal Confetti in Surprise Surprise

![Hard Candy Crystal Confetti in Surprise Surprise](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/20193848_8969_300x300.jpg)

Glitter nail polish is fun! You can where it alone or over another polish to enhance the color. Surprise Surprise from Hard Candy goes on smoothly and lasts. The color is fun and festive. The silver glitter polish reminds me of stars, snow, and moonlight. Find it for just $4.00.

## 6. Sally Hansen Insta Dri in Blazing Blue

![Sally Hansen Insta Dri in Blazing Blue](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/20193848_4682_224x212.jpg)

Sally Hansen Insta-Dri line has a wonderful color called Blazing Blue. I like dark blue polish, lots. Blazing Blue has a wonderful tone and shimmer. The deep color and shimmer remind me of glass ornaments and gift-wrapping paper. The best part is that it's just $3.00!

## 7. Maybelline Color Show in Wine and Dine

![Maybelline Color Show in Wine and Dine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/2013/11/20193848_3751_219x217.jpg)

The last color on our little list is this gorgeous Wine and Dine from Maybelline Color Show. This purple color is a wonderful deep color that is almost plum and looks like wine. The shimmer is lovely and the polish applies smoothly. We love this color – it’s great for the holiday season and beyond. At less than $4.00, you can stock up all year.

There are so many fun nail polish color options for the holiday season and beyond. These are just a few suggestions on affordable nail polish options for this holiday season. Prices in your area may vary slightly from the prices we note here. Do you have a staple color for the holiday? Please, share!
