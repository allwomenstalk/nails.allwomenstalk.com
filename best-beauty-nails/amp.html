Frequently Asked Questions

What are the latest nail trends for women?
Current trends include minimalist designs, pastel colors, and bold geometric patterns.
How can I make my nails grow faster and stronger?
Keep nails moisturized, avoid harsh chemicals, and eat a balanced diet rich in biotin.
What’s the best way to prevent nail breakage?
Use a nourishing base coat, avoid excessive water exposure, and trim nails regularly.
Are gel nails safe for my natural nails?
When applied and removed properly, gel nails are generally safe, but overuse can weaken nails.
How often should I get a manicure?
Every 1-2 weeks is ideal to maintain nail health and keep polish looking fresh.
What nail shapes are flattering for women?
Oval and almond shapes elongate fingers, while square shapes provide a modern look.
Can I do nail art at home easily?
Yes! Start with simple designs using nail stickers or dotting tools for beginner-friendly art.
How do I remove gel polish without damaging my nails?
Soak cotton pads in acetone, wrap them on nails with foil for 10-15 minutes, then gently remove.
What ingredients should I look for in nail care products?
Look for biotin, keratin, and vitamin E to strengthen and nourish nails.
How can I maintain healthy cuticles?
Keep them moisturized with cuticle oil and avoid cutting them to prevent infections.
