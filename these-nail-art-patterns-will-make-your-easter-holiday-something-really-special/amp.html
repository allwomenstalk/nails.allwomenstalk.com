23 Water Marble

Source: Zig Zag Watermarble - Trends
You will not be able to find any better colors for Easter than these.
The perfect watermarble blue is this Essie shimmer polish for $9.00 at shop.nordstrom.com

***

The water marble technique creates a stunning, rippled effect that's absolutely irresistible for festive occasions. When you combine the beauty of Easter pastels with the fun of water marbling, you create a look that's both playful and chic. To achieve this, simply drip drops of different pastel polishes into a bowl of water and use a toothpick to swirl the colors together. Dip your nails in, and like magic, you'll have a whimsical design that's sure to turn heads at any Easter brunch. Plus, it's incredibly satisfying to watch the pattern take shape - perfect for a crafty afternoon!
