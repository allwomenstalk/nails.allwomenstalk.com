# 7 Top Nail Polishes for Spring ...

One of my favorite things to do is to buy new nail polishes for spring and fall. I don’t know what it is about those two seasons in particular, but I just love stocking up on new colors that represent the season. If you’ve got a hankering to paint your fingers and toes with the newest nail polishes of the season, check out my picks for the 7 top nail polishes for spring!

## 1. Essie Walk down the Aisle

![Essie Walk down the Aisle](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nails/2013/04/1_essie-walk-down-the-aisle_300x300.jpg)

One of top trends in nail polishes for spring that was seen on runways was the pale nail. I used to shy away from white nail polish because it reminded me of White Out on my nails, but polish like Walk Down the Aisle is pale but still slightly sheer so it looks fresh, feminine and sophisticated for all sorts of outfits and occasions.

## 2. Butter LONDON Jasper

![Butter LONDON Jasper](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nails/2013/04/2_butter-london-jasper_295x450.jpg)

Another nail polish that I definitely want to add to my collection of spring nail polish is a cheery, pastel yellow. Yellow nail polish can be hard to pull off since you tend to only see neon, highlighter yellow or really sheer yellow that you can hardly see. This polish provides polish lovers with a happy medium and reminds me of sunshine!

## 3. OPI do You Lilac It?

![OPI do You Lilac It?](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nails/2013/04/3_opi-do-you-lilac-it_219x315.jpg)

Lavender is another gorgeous shade that’s a quintessential spring color! This hue kind of reminds me of Easter eggs but it makes for a really nice color for manicures and pedicures and showing off your spring sandals! This color also makes a good background color for fun and whimsical nail art or color blocking manicures.

## 4. Nails Inc. LONDON Kensington Palace Gardens

![Nails Inc. LONDON Kensington Palace Gardens](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nails/2013/04/4_nails-inc-london-kensington-palace-gardens_250x250.jpg)

I think we can all use a good pink nail polish anytime of the year! This girly pink-y/salmon color is perfect for spring and summer and will look great on a variety of skin tones. This is one of those hues that I see myself wearing over and over again because it’s such a fun color and it’ll match so many outfits!

## 5. OPI Suzy Skis in the Pyrenees

![OPI Suzy Skis in the Pyrenees](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nails/2013/04/5_opi-suzy-skis-in-the-pyrenees_500x500.jpg)

This nail polish might seem like an unlikely choice for a spring nail polish since it’s darker, but it was another hot nail color on the runways! If you’re not into pastels or just want to rock an edgier color when you go out for the evening, Suzy Skis in the Pyrenees is an awesome choice. This shade can easily be worn in the colder months, also!

## 6. Sally Hansen Xtreme Wear Nail Color Green with Envy

![Sally Hansen Xtreme Wear Nail Color Green with Envy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nails/2013/04/6_sally-hansen-xtreme-wear-nail-color-green-with-envy_410x410.jpg)

I know there are a lot of pastels and neutrals on this list so I wanted to add something a little more colorful and bright. Green with Envy looks a little yellowish in the photo but it goes on a true lime green. This is a fun and unexpected color to wear when you’re craving something different and unique to wear on your fingers!

## 7. China Glaze Re-fresh Mint

![China Glaze Re-fresh Mint](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/nails/2013/04/7_china-glaze-re-fresh-mint_400x400.jpg)

I can’t make a list on spring nail polish without including a mint green polish! Mint green is one of my favorite spring colors; I think I love it on pretty much everything, especially nails! This is a really charming color to wear on casual days or weekends and it looks great on so many skin tones!

Did you spot your favorite shade of spring nail polish? There are so many great colors and textures of nail polish these days that it makes it really hard to narrow down a few favorites! What colors are you wearing on your fingers and toes these days?
