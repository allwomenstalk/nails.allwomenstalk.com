Frequently Asked Questions

What are hot nails trends for this year?
Bold colors, chrome finishes, and intricate nail art designs are trending this year.
How often should I get a nail manicure?
Getting a manicure every 2-3 weeks helps maintain healthy, polished nails.
Can I do hot nail designs at home?
Yes, with the right tools like nail brushes and UV lamps, you can create trendy designs at home.
What nail shapes are popular right now?
Almond, coffin, and stiletto shapes are very popular among nail enthusiasts.
Are gel nails damaging?
When applied and removed properly, gel nails are safe, but frequent use without breaks can weaken nails.
How can I make my manicure last longer?
Use a good base and top coat, avoid harsh chemicals, and protect your nails when doing chores.
What colors are best for summer nails?
Bright shades like coral, neon pink, and turquoise are perfect for summer vibes.
Can nail art match my outfit?
Absolutely! Coordinating colors or themes in your nail art to your outfit adds a stylish touch.
What is the difference between gel and acrylic nails?
Gel nails use a UV light to cure polish, offering a flexible finish, while acrylics are a powder-liquid mix that hardens into a strong overlay.
How do I prevent nail infections?
Keep nails clean and dry, avoid biting, and don‚Äôt share nail tools to reduce infection risks.
