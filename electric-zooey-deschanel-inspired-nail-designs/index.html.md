# 7 Electric Zooey Deschanel Inspired Nail Designs ...

It goes without saying that Zooey Deschanel inspired nail designs are exceedingly popular. Known for her foxy red carpet manicures, Deschanel has been praised by critics and fans alike for her impeccable taste (in fashion and nail artists - celebrity nail technician Tom Bachik is the mastermind behind these genius designs). I love watching award ceremonies just to catch her latest fashion move. Trust me when I say that your inner fashionista will definitely fall for these electric Zooey Deschanel inspired nail designs.

## 1. Tuxedo Nail Art

At the 2012 Golden Globes Awards, this beloved actress raised the wow factor by sporting this sweet nail art; as a result, the tuxedo look is one of the trendiest Zooey Deschanel inspired nail designs. It doesn't come as a surprise that many tutorials have erupted from this fabulous design. YouTube sensation cute polish explains each step very well; she even adds in a little jacket to match the bow tie!

## 2. Daisy Nail Art

YouTube nail guru HelloMaphie recreates Deschanel's fantastic look at the 2014 Golden Globes. The nude polish in the background complements the white flower petals. Also, how could you not love that gold and glimmering center? Staring at this adorable daisy manicure makes me wish spring would come a little faster!

## 3. Television Nail Art

Could there be anything greater than this clever nail art? Zooey Deschanel rocked this gorgeous manicure to the 2012 Emmy Awards. The bold contrast between the gold and black nail polish makes this design that much more promising. With PackAPunchPolish as your guide, you'll perfect this look in no time!

## 4. Checkered Nail Art

Zooey Deschanel certainly rocked the house with this striking geometrically patterned manicure at the 2013 Emmy Awards. Monochromatic designs are one of my favorite trends this season. YouTube user LizzyTheDIYLady totally nailed this tutorial! I love the use of the dots as the finishing touch.

## 5. Movie Reel Nail Art

At the 2013 Golden Globes, Zooey Deschanel brought some serious competition to the nail game. As an ode to the nominated films, Deschanel sported a classy film nail design. Complete with a mini camera, this manicure is highly innovative with just the right amount of quirkiness. Her website, HelloGiggles, created an easy to follow tutorial that I am definitely more than grateful for.

## 6. Christmas Sweater Nail Art

Although the holidays are over, there's no denying that this is simply one of the cutest manicures around. I never thought that I would love this design as much as I do now. YouTube nail artist Camille Anne did a wonderful job at replicating this gorgeous look.

## 7. Colorblock Nail Art

Lastly, this colorblock manicure by MexiBelen actually combines two looks into one stunning image. Pairing these two shades together resulted in an absolutely amazing design! When I first stumbled on this tutorial, I automatically thought of the time when Zooey Deschanel posted a picture of her purple and lavendar manicure on her instagram account, as well as a similar look using the colors mint and nude. Overall, the best thing about this twist on the classic french manicure is that it is easy to achieve. Sometimes, keeping your nails simple is a better option.

Zooey Deschanel should just be crowned the queen of the manicure runway. What are your favorite looks rocked by this actress? Which celebrity nails are you looking forward to seeing at upcoming award shows?
