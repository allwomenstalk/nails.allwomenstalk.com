# The Best Matte Manicure Inspo for Any and All Seasons ...

Are you seeing **matte manicure ideas** all over social media?

If you pay more attention to the photos you are viewing, you will realize that this kind of manicure is totally dominating right now.

Whether you're on Instagram, Pinterest, Twitter, Facebook…this manicure is on every girl's nails.

If you want your nails to be on point, you need **matte manicure inspo.** This is the hottest new trend and you are going to love it!

## 1. BLACK MATTE

![nail, finger, hand, manicure, nail care,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/up/vz/ajr4kfym599dd35969334172246457_600x648.jpg)

Your nails should be short, squared and polished with a matte black nail polish for a refined and rebellious look, and honestly one of the **best matte manicures** you'll see anywhere.

## 2. ALMOND MATTE

![finger, nail, hand, manicure, nail care,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/mv/fe/b8zi4hw1599ddc61cd36d134819056_480x480.jpg)

Or just keep that gentle and feminine look with long, rounded nail tips polished in a neutral shade.

## 3. MATTE LINES

![nail, finger, hand, nail care, manicure,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vl/vz/mf6hx7x1599ddc8d00f1c880631403_500x499.jpg)

Combine your matte nail polish with glossy golden lines to make your handshakes more impressive.

## 4. COLORFUL MATTE

![nail, finger, hand, nail care, manicure,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n7/a2/axtdwbqw599ee3987a4a1451023557_600x600.jpg)

These colorful matte nails are great for expressing your positive energy.

## 5. MATTE in 2 COLORS

![nail, finger, nail care, nail polish, hand,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/d7/lg/g1l32xkg599ee4903c285703326160_600x600.jpg)

Its best to stick to a line split in half to create an illusion of longer nails.

## 6. MATTE with DOTS

![nail, finger, pink, hand, nail care,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/dw/ic/svvb5jag599ee4c52a06d691014203_600x600.jpg)
