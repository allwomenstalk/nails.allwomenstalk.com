# Best Tips for Classy Nails ...

Nice nails are essential if you want to look well groomed. Nothing says classy like a set of perfectly manicured and polished nails! If you want a well-kempt, well-finished look, there are several points to bear in mind in order to achieve nice nails. So here are some **tips for classy nails**.

## 1. USE a QUALITY POLISH

The first of my **tips for classy nails** is to use a good quality polish. Cheaper polishes are fun to experiment with, but they don´t last as well as better quality and more pricey brands. Some things are definitely worth paying more for! Buy a few test bottles from a more expensive brand and you´ll find that the colour lasts much longer before you need to reapply, or it begins to chip.

## 2. BE CAREFUL with APPLICATION

When you want nice looking nails, it´s vital to take your time in applying polish. As with mascara, it’s a guarantee that if you rush it when putting on polish you will make a mess of it! Apply carefully and slowly, allowing enough time for it to dry. Have a cotton bud soaked in polish remover on hand to remove any smudges.

## 3. Do a GOOD MANICURE

For really nice nails, you need to prepare your nails in the same way that you cleanse and prime your face. Give your nails a good manicure before applying polish. Firstly, clean off all traces of old polish and then, tidy up the cuticles. Lastly, file your nails with a good quality emery board. They’re now ready for some lovely new polish!

## 4. BE SENSIBLE with COLOUR CHOICE

Classy nails require a careful choice of colour. Neon green may be fun for going out clubbing, but it can hardly be described as classy! Classy means being somewhat conservative in your choice. Stick to colours like deep red, silver, or soft pink. The same goes for nail art – classy is no time to be creative!

## 5. MAKE SURE IT’S a CHIP-FREE ZONE!

One of the most important tips for classy nails is that there must be absolutely NO chips! As soon as that polish chips, it´s time to reapply. Don´t try touching it up, as it will be obvious. Remove the polish and start again by applying a new coat. If necessary, do the full set again.

## 6. HAVE a GOOD FOUNDATION to WORK on

Like I mentioned, your nails need preparation just like your face before applying makeup. For a lasting and smart polish application, you need to apply a base coat. This helps the polish to adhere but also protects your nails from staining. Add the colour polish and once it has dried, apply a top coat and your nails will stay looking stunning for longer.

## 7. TREAT YOUR HANDS and NAILS GENTLY

If you want to keep your nails looking great, you need to avoid any manual tasks. The two things simply do not mix. When you have beautifully manicured and polished nails, keep them that way by being very careful and above all, don´t use your nails as tools!
.
Nice nails take a bit of looking after but it´s worth the effort. You feel so smart and elegant when you have perfectly shaped nails with smart polish! Make sure that you feed your nails with lots of hand cream in between polish applications so that they stay in good condition.
