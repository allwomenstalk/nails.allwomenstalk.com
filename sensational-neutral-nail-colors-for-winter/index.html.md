# Sensational Neutral Nail Colors for Winter ...

When you think of winter [nail colors](https://nails.allwomenstalk.com/unique-nail-colors-sure-to-get-attention/), what comes to mind? Dark, vampy, or maybe wearing no polish at all? Just because the season changes doesn't mean you have to resort to the same old boring nail polish shades. Since it's darker and colder, that makes it even more necessary to spice things up with some interesting nail colors. Let us show you some fantastic neutral nail colors that are far from ordinary to take your winter nail game from blah to beautiful!

## 1. Sally Hansen Color Therapy in Make My Clay

![Nail, nail polish, nail care, cosmetics, hand,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/yd/w0/lgmoo48k586a8e3eaa79b095911170_450x440.jpg)

Meet the newest good-for-your-nails polish that contains Argan oil to moisturize and nourish your nails. In addition to the added moisture, it comes in 38 flattering shades that last up to 10 days. This calming beige shade is perfect for everyday wear to work or class.

Price: $6.99 at [target.com](http://www.target.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 2. Formula X Nail Polish in Gray Matter

![nail polish, nail care, cosmetics, eye, organ,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vw/t3/tfoshiie586a8e3eef2b9168888389_426x534.jpg)

Charcoal gray is ideal for those who don't like black nail polish but want to rock dark nails. It's a little edgy, chic, and will make your nails stand out in the best way. You'll love how your nails will be saturated with color.

Price: $10.50 at [sephora.com](http://www.sephora.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 3. OPI Nail Lacquer in Stranger Tides

_ At first glance, you might think I'm a bit cray cray for suggesting a nail polish that slightly resembles pureed parsley. However, if you love mint green polish, this is the makes a wonderful option for the winter.

Price: $7.79 at target.com_

## 4. Zoya Nail Lacquer in Chantal

![nail polish, nail care, cosmetics, glass bottle, perfume,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/i6/vz/y5cqfy5b586a8e3f4b96d204599635_400x352.jpg)

Despite how the color looks in the bottle, this neutral nail polish is quite opaque on your nails and complements many skin tones. Personally, I think it's the best way to dress up your nails without going too crazy with color.

Price: $9 at [ulta.com](http://www.ulta.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 5. Essie Nail Color Comfy in Cashmere

![Essie, nail polish, nail care, cosmetics, eye,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/n1/sr/esf3t0x4586a8e3f940f0043599922_500x297.jpg)

Price: $9 at [essie.com](https://www.essie.com/base-and-top-coats/effects)

## 6. China Glaze in White on White

![China Glaze, nail polish, nail care, cosmetics, finger,](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cm/pq/p4hnrjpk586a8e3fd3914588433139_600x377.jpg)

Price: $6.99 at [sallybeauty.com](http://www.sallybeauty.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)

## 8. OPI Infinite Shine 2 Lacquer in Olive for Green

## 9. Butter London Pantone Color of the Year Lacquer in Caramel Cafe

## 10. Julep Nail Color in Rooney
