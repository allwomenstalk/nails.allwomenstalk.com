# Whiten Your Discolored Nails with These Simple Home Remedies ...

Discolored nails aren’t pretty and if you don’t want to have to wear polish every day, you can think about whitening them. Then even if all you do is file and buff them to a shine, they’ll still be pretty and will look healthy too. Try these home remedies to whiten your nails:

## 1. The Power of Lemons

This simple remedy works because lemon has bleaching properties. That makes it an ideal way to get rid of yellow stains you have on your nails. Get a bowl full of fresh lemon juice and soak your nails in it for 15 minutes. Use a soft toothbrush to scrub your nails and remove those yellow stains. Rinse your hands with lukewarm water after the treatment. Repeat twice a day and it will prove to be one of the easiest ways to whiten your nails.

## 2. Use Hydrogen Peroxide

You can use 3% hydrogen peroxide solution on discolored nails because it possesses impressive whitening properties. Take one-half cup of water and add four tablespoons of hydrogen peroxide to it. Mix it well and then soak your nails in it for a couple of minutes. Scrub your nails using a soft toothbrush and wash them with clean water. Use a moisturizing lotion afterwards to avoid dehydrating your nails.

## 3. Magical Baking Soda

Baking soda comes with exfoliating and bleaching properties, so it is an effective way to get rid of yellow stains from your nails. Make a paste by mixing a teaspoon of lemon juice, one-half teaspoon of olive oil, and a tablespoon of baking soda. Apply this paste to your nails using a soft toothbrush. Rub your nails gently for about 5-6 minutes and then wash them with warm water. Repeat every two weeks for good results.

## 4. Ever Thought of Using Orange Peel?

Rubbing your nails with fresh orange peel twice a day will improve the color of your nails. It works because it is rich in vitamin C and helps get rid of yellow stains effectively. You can even use powdered orange peel mixed in water for good effects.

## 5. An Unusual Use for Toothpaste

If your toothpaste can whiten your teeth, it can do the same for your nails. Your whitening toothpaste, which contains hydrogen peroxide and other whitening ingredients, will work quite well for mild stains. Apply some paste on your nails and leave it there for 15 minutes. Now, scrub your nails with the help of a nailbrush. Then, use cotton balls soaked in lukewarm water to wash the toothpaste off completely. Repeat twice a week for a month.

## 6. And by the Same Principle, Use Denture Cleaning Pills

Denture cleaning pills will help whiten your nails because they contain citric acid and sodium bicarbonate. Dissolve a couple of pills in a bowl of lukewarm water and then soak your hands in this mixture, After 15 minutes, pat your hands dry, and use a moisturizing lotion to avoid dryness. Repeat thrice a week for a couple of months.

## 7. Try Tea Tree Oil

Sometimes, yellow nails are due to a fungal infection. If that's the case, using tea tree oil is one of the best remedies. Thanks to its anti-fungal, antimicrobial, and antiseptic properties, it eliminates infection and whitens your nails quickly. Use an eyedropper to apply oil directly to your nail beds. Leave there for a few minutes and wash it off with lukewarm water. Repeat twice a day for a few months.

None of these home remedies to whiten nails work with one application. You need to persevere for a few weeks – bit it will be worth it? Will you be giving any of these a go?
