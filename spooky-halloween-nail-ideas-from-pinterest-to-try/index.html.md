# Spooky Halloween Nail Ideas to Try from Pinterest ...

If Halloween costumes aren't really your thing, how about just decorating your nails? It can still make quite the impact! Check out some awesome Halloween nail ideas from Pinterest that you can try out:

## 1. Splattered Blood

![Splattered Blood](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vw/yu/pd3ar29a_585x788.jpg)

Crime scene nails? SO awesome.

## 2. Scarecrow Mani

![Scarecrow Mani](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/vv/zf/l298nv52_475x705.jpg)

Put a twist on this classic french manicure with an orange tip!

## 3. Frankenstein

![Frankenstein](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ab/bf/f8z80n2b_600x738.jpg)

I'm obsessed these green little monsters!

## 4. Spooky Pumpkins

![Spooky Pumpkins](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ma/wc/qweardef_375x500.jpg)

You might need really long nails for this one!

## 5. Spider Web

![Spider Web](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rw/uv/nz3ucix5_433x640.jpg)

I love the idea of dedicating just one nail to the spider web and having the rest of the nails a solid color.

## 6. Ghosts

![Ghosts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/hv/nh/rjlvtmlt_451x668.jpg)

Whether they're friendly or spooky, ghosts are an awesome nail art addition.

## 7. Skulls

![Skulls](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/a5/vn/j4fj4jpu_400x561.jpg)

Use a nail art pen to create those fine thin lines!

Which of these is your favorite? How will you be decorating your nails for Halloween this year?
