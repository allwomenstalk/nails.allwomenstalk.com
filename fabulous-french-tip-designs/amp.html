***

This beautiful nail look combines two of the hottest trends in nail art: nude nails and black French tips. The combination of the two creates a classic and timeless look that will never go out of style. The addition of holographic glitter accents adds a modern touch that will make your nails stand out.

The nude nails provide a neutral canvas that can be dressed up with the black French tips. The tips are painted on with a thin brush and can be made as thin or thick as desired. The holographic glitter accents provide a subtle sparkle to the nails that will make them really stand out.

To complete the look, a clear top coat is applied over the French tips and holographic glitter accents. This will help to protect the design and keep it looking great for longer.

This look is perfect for any occasion, from a night out with friends to a formal event. It is also a great way to add a touch of glamour to your everyday look. The best part is that it is easy to do at home and won’t take too much time. All you need are some nude nail polish, black nail polish, holographic glitter, and a clear top coat.
