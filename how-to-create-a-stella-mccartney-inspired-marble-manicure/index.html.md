# How to Create a Stella McCartney Inspired Marble Manicure ...

Would you love to give yourself a marble manicure? It's easy! According to [Elle,](https://www.elle.com/beauty/news/g27964/stella-mccartney-nails-fall-2016/) here are the steps to creating Stella McCartney inspired nails:

## 1. Create a Burgundy Base

![Create a Burgundy Base](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/rr/ew/kcpb2u9m56e02574b66e8822833120_480x640.jpg)

Start by painting your nails a solid color.

## 2. Apply a Top Coat

![Apply a Top Coat](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/w9/za/ozkyvlcs56e02574e3764722221331_480x720.jpg)

Make sure you apply a top coat over your base polish to keep it in place.

## 3. Dot the Nail with Red Polish

![Dot the Nail with Red Polish](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ja/l7/aglmymdl56e0257501eba474112130_480x640.jpg)

Now it's time to apply miniature dashes all over your nail.

## 4. Dot with a Light Blue Polish

![Dot with a Light Blue Polish](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/j7/ya/cxopmvzi56e0257532be1488717626_480x720.jpg)

Grab a different polish and make even more dashes on your nails.

Would you wear your nails like this?
