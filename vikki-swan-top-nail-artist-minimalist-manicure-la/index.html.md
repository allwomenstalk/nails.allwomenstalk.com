# Vikki Swan \- The Nail Artist Redefining Global Beauty Standards from Her LA Studio

In the **nail industry**, where **attention to detail and flawless shapes are highly valued**, the name **Victoriia Lebedinskaia** — [Vikki Swan](https://vikkiswan.pro/)— is well known to many professionals around the world. Based in Los Angeles, Vikki is best known as a **master of minimalist manicures** that focus on perfect nail architecture, crisp French lines, and clean square shapes.

Over **more than eight years in the industry**, Vikki has grown from a beginner nail technician in Russia to **one of the strongest specialists**, sought after not only by clients but also by the professional community. Her key to success is a strict approach to **quality and the ability to perfect the shape.** She doesn’t chase complex designs; instead, she shows how minimalism and cleanliness can become **true art**.

![](https://img.allw.mn/415a29b4d92f7812ebf008aafd7a23_1024x1454.jpg)

Throughout these years, **she has won over 50 titles at international championships**, proving that a flawless French manicure and perfect squares are a competitive advantage on any stage. Among her notable achievements is her absolute victory at the **Brilliant World Cup in Dubai in 2025**, where she took **first place in all 12 manicure categories**. That same year, Vikki won the **Grand Prix — 1st Place in the Professional Instructor category at the Hollywood Brilliant Beauty World Cup**, confirming her top level as both a master and an educator.

![](https://img.allw.mn/31e50a63cd1f187ab0b006a6719e62_1024x1425.jpg)

Today, she works in **her own studio in Los Angeles**, where she emphasizes a personalized approach and meticulous work with nail shape. Her clients are people who value neat manicures, classic French designs, and **strict adherence to high standards**.

In addition to her practice, **Vikki runs an international online nail academy** with training available in English, Russian, and Spanish. Throughout her career, she has **personally trained over 500 nail technicians** through live classes and online programs — and she continues to share her experience and methods through her online academy. Her goal is not only to teach techniques but to **pass on her approach to perfect nail architecture**, which **she developed through years of practice and championship wins**.

![](https://img.allw.mn/2b679f458d1ffb788c73e93871e5bc_1024x1684.jpg)

As a **certified instructor and judge**, she actively contributes to the **growth of the industry**, **evaluates work at international competitions, and conducts both live and online training**.

**Vikki Swan** is proof that the beauty of a manicure can be **minimalist, precise, and impeccable**. She shows that a perfectly crafted shape and a clean French design can become a **nail artist’s signature** and elevate them to the world stage.

![](https://img.allw.mn/d557c0b0e92c96dbf19c2d3949e79d_1024x1391.jpg)

**From Russia to Los Angeles** — her journey **inspires nail artists** who choose clean work and discipline over complicated designs and fleeting trends. And that’s exactly why **her name today is associated with high quality and true professionalism.**
